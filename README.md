# Business Analytics Dashboard – Power BI

This project presents a **comprehensive Business Analytics dashboard** developed in Power BI to analyze financial performance, sales trends, marketing insights, and supply chain efficiency. The report contains multiple interactive views with KPIs, visuals, and dynamic filters to support business decision-making.

## Dashboard Structure

The report includes **6 interactive tabs**:

### 1. Home Page

* Navigation buttons to access different analytical views.

### 2. Finance View

* **KPIs:** Net Sales, Gross Margin %, Net Profit %
* **Filters:** Region & Market, Segment & Category, Customer
* **Slicers:** Month, Quarter, YTD/YTG, vs LY/vs Target
* **Visuals:**

  * Line chart showing sales performance over time
  * P&L statement (Final Value, YoY Change, YoY %) in a matrix visual
  * Top & Bottom products and customers based on sales

### 3. Sales View

* **KPI:** Sales Quantity
* **Filters:** Region & Market, Segment & Category, Customer
* **Slicers:** Month, Quarter, YTD/YTG
* **Visuals:**

  * Dot chart showing Gross Margin and Net Sales across regions
  * Matrix with key metrics (Segment, Net Sales, Gross Margin, GM %)
  * Customer performance metrics (NS, GM, GM %, Quantity)
  * Donut chart for Net Sales / Gross Margin by customers

### 4. Marketing View

* **Filters:** Region & Market, Segment & Category, Customer
* **Slicers:** Month, Quarter, YTD/YTG
* **Visuals:**

  * Dot chart showing GM / Net Sales across regions
  * Matrix showing regional metrics (NS, GM, GM %, NP, NP %)
  * Segment-level performance metrics
  * Donut chart showing Gross Margin and Profit/Loss by products

### 5. Supply Chain View

* **KPIs:** Forecast Accuracy, Net Error, ABS Error
* **Filters:** Region & Market, Segment & Category, Customer
* **Slicers:** Month, Quarter, YTD/YTG
* **Visuals:**

  * Scatter chart showing Net Error and Forecast Accuracy over time
  * Customer-level supply chain metrics (Forecast Accuracy, NE, NE %, Risk)
  * Product-level analysis including Forecast Accuracy %, LY comparison, and Net Error

### 6. Executive View

* A summarized overview of key business metrics to support high-level decision making.

## Purpose

This dashboard provides a **360° view of business performance**, enabling analysis of financial results, sales performance, marketing effectiveness, and supply chain accuracy.
